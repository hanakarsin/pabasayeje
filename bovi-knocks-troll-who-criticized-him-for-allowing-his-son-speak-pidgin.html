Comedian Bovi Knocks Troll Who Criticized Him For Allowing His Son Speak Pidgin

Publish date: 2024-06-28

Nigerian comedian cum actor Bovi Ugbomma has knocked a troll who criticized him for allowing his son to speak pidgin.

Bovi had taken to his Instagram page to share a video of his son playing outside and he decided to hail him asking how he was faring.

He said: “Chuchu, How you de?” and his son responded, “I dey fine”

However, a troll who seemed it was out of place to use such language for his child tackled Bovi saying it is only in Africa that a father would be proud that his child can’t speak his native language.

The troll added that many Africans are still slaves.

The statement didn’t sit well with Bovi as he slammed the troll and called him a fool.

Repfrica wrote: “is only in Africa a father will be proud Cous his child can speak his native language. We are still slaves”

Bovi wrote: “And you write this English, What a fool”
