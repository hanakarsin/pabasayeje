Singer Tems' Fierce Fan Sternly Warns Wizkid Over Viral Video, Says 'Let It Be The Last Time'

Publish date: 0001-01-01

A female fan who assumed the role of a ‘mother’ in the life of Nigerian singer, Tems has taken to microblogging platform, Twitter to sternly warn Afrobeat star, Wizkid over his failed attempt to lift the female singer up during their live performance at just concluded ‘MADE IN LAGOS’ concert held at London 02 Arena. 

Naija News reports that Wizkid, sparked reactions on social media after a video of him trying to lift Tems while performing in the O2 Arena, London, surfaced online.

However, Tems carefully brought down his hands, disengaging from him, thus not allowing Wizkid to carry her up.

This has led to a series of mixed reactions from Nigerians on social media.

Amongst the reactions, is an appalled female fan who was quick to assume the role of the female singer’s online mother, warning Wizkid not to ever put his hand on Tems again.

According to her, when Wizkid performed with Burna boy, he did not make an attempt to lift her up.

Yvicue wrote; ‘@Wizkidayo let this be the last time you toch Tems on stage. I didn’t see you rubbing on Burna Biy. Let this be the last time.’
