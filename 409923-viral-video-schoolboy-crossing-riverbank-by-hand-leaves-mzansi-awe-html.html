Viral Video of Schoolboy Crossing River Using His Hands Leaves Netizens in Awe

Publish date: 2024-07-12

- A video of a young schoolboy crossing a slippery river using his hands has gone viral on social media

- The short clip has been watched more than 720k times already and has left many social media users astonished

The ingenuity of young Africans faced with challenging circumstances never ceases to amaze the world.

A young boy has inspired social media users with his athleticism and skill after a video of him crossing a muddy river using his hands went viral on Facebook.

In the short clip, the talented boy still dressed in school uniform begins by tilting into a graceful handstand.

Without a lot of effort, he crosses the muddy riverbank in less than a minute.
