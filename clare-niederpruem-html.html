Clare Niederpruem - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-07-19

Perhaps most well known for her portrayal of Bree Carrington in the 2015 film Once I Was a Beehive, she has other film credits in Thirst and The Mentor.

Before Fame

She was featured in stage productions of Little Women and 110 in the Shade at the University of Tampa.

Trivia

She has served as a costume designer on several of the films she has acted in, including SAGA: Curse of the Shadow, The Crown and the Dragon, Dragonfyre and Dudes & Dragons.

Family Life

She grew up with three brothers.

Associated With

She starred alongside Allie Jennings in the movie Once I Was a Beehive.
