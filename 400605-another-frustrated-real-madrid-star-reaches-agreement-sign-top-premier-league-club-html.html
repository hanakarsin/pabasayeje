Real Madrids Martin Odegaard close to joining Arsenal on loan

Publish date: 2024-06-16

- Martin Odegaard's loan move to Arsenal from Real Madrid is at its completion stage

- The Danish playmaker has failed to nail a starting place in Zinedine Zidane's side

- The 22-year-old year star will become the third player to leave Los Blancos to the Premier League this season

Martin Odegaard is set to become the third Real Madrid player to Premier League club as the Danish midfielder is close to sealing a loan deal to Arsenal.

The 22-year-old has managed to be in Zinedine Zidane's starting XI in five matches after returning from a successful loan spell at Real Sociedad.

The midfielder was recalled from his loan spell at the end of last season after he flourished under Sociedad boss Imanol Alguacil.
