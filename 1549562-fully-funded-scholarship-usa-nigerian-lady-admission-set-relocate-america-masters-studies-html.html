Publish date: 2024-08-04
  • A Nigerian lady is set to begin her master's programme at the University of Nebraska, Omaha, USA

A lady got a scholarship to study for a master's degree at the University of Nebraska in Omaha, USA.

The lady, Cynthia Nukege, said she got the fully-funded scholarship five years after getting her first degree.

She shared the good news with her followers on Twitter.
