'This Is Unnecessary, Be Grateful'

Publish date: 2024-08-21

Nigerian crossdresser James Obialor popularly called James Brown has come under attack for publicly blasting his mentor and colleague Bobrisky in a recent interview.

In the interview with popular media personality Chude Jidenowo, James Brown urged Bobrisky to stop taking credit for his successful lifestyle and journey to fame.

James Brown opined that Bobrisky did not make him simply because of a hundred thousand naira cash gift that he gave him.

According to James Brown, the N100K gift did nothing to him as he gave it to his father to sort his N1.2 million bail condition.

James Brown added that many people also gifted him but didn’t announce it like Bobrisky and can return the money in ten folds if he wants it back.

The crossdresser noted that he’s grateful for what Bobrisky did for him but he shouldn’t rub it on his face at all times.

This sparked mixed reactions online, while some netizens took sides with James Brown others attacked him over the comment.
