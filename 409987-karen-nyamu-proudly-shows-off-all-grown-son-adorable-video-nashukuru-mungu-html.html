Budding politician Karen Nyamu has excited her social media followers after sharing a cute video of her already grown baby Sam Junior.

The city lawyer and Kikuyu musician Samidoh have been in the recent past involved in public drama concerning the state of their relationship.

In the video, Nyamu showed her baby, who looks exactly like his dad, sitting on the couch, shouting and calling for his mother's attention.

Nyamu captioned the post saying that the kid loves attention just like the mother.

The video was shared by @urbannews254 on Instagram.
