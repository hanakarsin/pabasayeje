Nyota Ndogo Tells Off Haters Claiming She's Desperate for Mzungu Hubby's Love: &quot;Ondoka&quot;

Publish date: 2024-03-12
  • The Watu na Viatu crooner disclosed that most of her haters are men who have been trolling her for getting back with her hubby
  • Nyota noted that some of her critics claimed she looks desperate trying to win her hubby over
  • She said that she will be dealing with trolls head-on and that they should be ready for clap backs whenever they attack her

Celebrated Kenyan songstress Nyota Ndogo has slammed critics who have been on her neck with claims that she has been pulling desperate moves in a bid to win over her Danish hubby, Nielsen.

Jealous male fans

According to Nyota Ndogo, most of the people who have been criticising her on social media over her relationship with hubby are men.

In a long Instagram post seen by TUKO.co.ke on Saturday, October 2, the mother of two wondered why most men were sending her hate after her reunion with Nielsen.

Nyota said she suspects such men who have been calling her names for getting back with her hubby are just jealous and maybe wish they had her.

The star blatantly asked her male haters to keep off her page if they feel she is doing everything wrong even as she tries to rekindle her romance with boo.

"I have gone through the comments and it is sad to report that most are negative. Some people claiming I am acting so desperate while trying to rekindle the flame with my hubby. Most negative comments come from men. You keep trolling me but still, stalk me through my pages. It is like they never wanted my hubby to come back. I think they are just mad and jealous that me and hubby are back together," Nyota wrote.
