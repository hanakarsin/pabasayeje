Singer Blaqbonez Rocks Teni During Stage Performance In London, Nigerians React

Publish date: 2024-06-10

Nigerian singer, Blaqbonez has sparked mixed reactions online for rocking his senior colleague, Teni, during a stage performance in London.

Naija News reports that the fast-rising singer and Teni thrilled the audience during his debut headline concert in London on Friday, November 11, 2022.

A video shared online captured the singers showing off their dance skills as Teni delivers one of his hit songs.

The singers could pass as lovers in the viral video as Blaqbonez wraps his arms around Teni and they both sway side to side for a moment.

The video sparked mixed reactions from netizens, while some made fun of the fast-rising singer others claimed he is the only singer that can do such a thing to Teni.
