Singer Vinka shares cute photos of her introduction ceremony on Instagram

Publish date: 2024-07-18

- After Vinka gave birth to a baby girl and she decided to take the next step in her relationship.

- She recently introduced the father of her child to her parents for confirmation during the introduction ceremony.

- Vinka still cannot be referred to as someone's wife until she's taken the necessary steps required for her to attain the title.

Veronica Luggya also known as Vinka recently posted pictures of her introduction ceremony to Instagram to symbolise that she is no longer in the 'market'.

After having given birth to a bouncing beautiful baby girl, Vinka took the time to participate in the Ugandan Kwanjula ceremony where she introduced the love of her life and father of her child to her parents and other family members.

The Ugandan ceremony Kwanjula (introduction) can best be described as an introductory marriage ceremony where a young girl takes her husband-to-be to her parents for approval before moving to the next step.

We have edited the post to "taken" as kukyala can't determine a person's marital status, although it is a very important step towards making it official. Kwanjula(Traditional wedding ), a religious or civil wedding are the ceremonies recognised by law as gateways to marriage."

It was made clear that the Kwajula ceremony she took part in did not mean she was married.

It just meant that her parents have approved of the man she wants to marry.

The commentators expressed that Vinka still has a few steps to take before she is referred to as a wife.
