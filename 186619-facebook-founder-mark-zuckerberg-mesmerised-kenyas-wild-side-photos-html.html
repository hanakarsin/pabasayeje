Mark Zuckerberg blown away by Kenya's nature

Publish date: 2024-02-24

- Facebook founder and CEO Mark Zuckerberg who is on a surprise tour to Kenya took time off to sample country's wild side and he was totally blown away 

Any person visiting Kenya can never go back home without sampling our country's beautiful nature and especially our wildlife. This always leaves them enchanted!

Facebook founder and CEO Mark Zuckerberg was also charmed by Kenya's wild side and shared some pictures of what he saw and fell in love with.

He spent a better part of his sight-seeing in Lake Naivasha where he even took a boat ride and totally fell in love with the country.

The Facebook CEO is in a surprise visit to the country where he is meeting entrepreneurs and learn about mobile money.

Zuckerberg melted the hearts of Kenyans after he took fish and ugali for lunch.

The Facebook CEO  took lunch at Mama Oliech's restaurant at Yaya centre in Nairobi, shocking many.

He founded Facebook at 23 years of age and is now the 6th richest man in the world.
