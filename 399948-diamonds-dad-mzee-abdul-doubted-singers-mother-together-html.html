Diamond's dad Mzee Abdul says he always doubted singer's mother when they were together

Publish date: 2024-04-08

- Mzee Abdul came out to address the controversies concerning his relationship with Diamond Platnumz

- He said he always doubted the star's mother even when they were together but expressed gratitude for eventually knowing the truth

- However, he has said he will sue his son should he continue using his name as his official name

Looks like more drama is about to unfold following Diamond's dad, Mzee Abdul's rebuttal on the singer's mother's claims that he is not the star's biological father.

The dad appeared on a YouTube interview with Rick Media where he said, he no longer wants people to refer to him as diamond's father.

He also disclosed a lot of information concerning his relationship with ex-lover Sandra, who is the Wasafi records owner's mother.

Diamond's mother Sandra posted on her Instagram page revealing who the real biological father of the star was, and it was not Mzee Abdul as it was widely known.

While giving his redress on the paternity issue that has been doing rounds on social media, he said:

''What she said has made me happy, I am glad and relieved. This is because there were so many things I kept asking myself, like why is it that when I want something I cannot get it but when others ask, they end up getting it? Turns out, she and her son sat down behind my back and planned their own things and now I got the answers in this short time, that I am not Diamond's biological dad.''

He also expressed gratitude for the Baba Lao hit song maker's mother for publicly being open and honest concerning that matter.

He also noted that he will sue the artist if he continues to use his name.

Diamond Platnum's real name is Naseeb Abdul Juma, which he got from Mzee Abdul and now, he says, because he was not consulted before the news went public, he has also stated in the open that he will sue his son should he continue to use his name.

''I am glad that now this issue can be put to rest, Tanzanians kept asking why i was spotted carrying loads and living like a pauper, now they also get their answers, I am not a beneficiary in that family as I am not the biological dad.''

He however, criticized Sandra for coming open to the public like she did considering this was a private family matter. He added:

''This woman has failed a bit, she revealed to her son that I am not his real dad now when he is an adult. She should have done when he was still very young. There were many things that came up when we were together, like living with her, taking her to hospital when she was pregnant till she gave birth, raising him and now when the son has gained riches, it's when she breaks the news to him and here I was asking for help little did I know I was not a beneficiary.''
