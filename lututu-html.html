LuTuTu - Trivia, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-08-15

Gaming content creator and YouTube personality who rose to fame through the use of his self-titled channel. He publishes Roblox roleplaying content based on the mod Brookhaven. He has more than 2.2 million subscribers. 

Before Fame

One of his oldest archived uploads is the 2021 Minecraft video, "Traps for Spongebob and Friends in Minecraft Complilation." 

Trivia

His "RP - FUNNY MOMENTS (ROBBER) ALL EPISODES" Roblox video went viral with more than 31 million views in total. Outside of traditional video content, he also creates shorts. 

Family Life

He was born in the United States. 

Associated With

He plays Roblox, a game developed by Notch.
