2. Bantu knots

Bantu knots are small coiled buns. This is one of the most exotic hairstyles from Africa. The hair is separated into several sections, each of which is then done into a knot in a specific way.

It is a bold style that serves as a perfect protected look for ladies who want their hair entirely up and out of the way. This hairstyle doesn't damage your hair but helps you keep it neat and very stylish.

Twists are a type of protective hairstyle that also happens to be regarded as a low manipulation hairstyle because it does not require a lot of work. The twist style is simple and can be done on your own. This twisted hairdo looks great on both short and long hair.
