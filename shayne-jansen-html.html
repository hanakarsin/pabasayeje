Shayne Jansen - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-05-25

Reality TV star known for appearing on season two of Love Is Blind. His shaynejansen Instagram account promotes his TV appearances and features photos from his daily life. He has amassed 90,000 followers on the platform.

Before Fame

He played baseball and football during his secondary school years. He went on to attend the University of Wisconsin, Stevens Point.       

Trivia

He is a real estate broker whose business is based in Chicago, Illinois. In March of 2022, he revealed in an interview that he and costar Natalie Lee are no longer on good terms. 

Family Life

He is from De Pere, Wisconsin. His father, Wayne Jansen, died in March of 2021. 

Associated With

Amber Pike was a prominent cast member on season one of Love is Blind.
