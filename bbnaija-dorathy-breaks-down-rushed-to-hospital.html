Dorathy Breaks Down, Rushed To Hospital

Publish date: 2024-04-01

First runner-up in the Big Brother Naija House, Dorathy Bachor, on Friday morning was rushed to the hospital.

The development was disclosed by her sister, Cynthia Bachor, on her Twitter page after viewers complained of her absence from a media rounds earlier today.

Cynthia in her post noted that Dorathy broke down and was rushed to the hospital for treatment.

From the picture posted online there are signs that she was given a drip at the hospital.

Her tweet read: “Rushing her to the hospital in the early hours of today is definitely the scariest thing that ever happened to me.”

“Pray for my baby to be back on her feet soon. Thank you guys for all the messages.”
