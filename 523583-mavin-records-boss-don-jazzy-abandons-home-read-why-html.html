Don Jazzy Abandons Home- Read Why

Publish date: 2024-05-30

Don Jazzy again! The Mavin Records boss is one music producer that is known to have the Midas touch as most of the songs from his label are hits.

And for every success story there is always a price to it. For the love of what he is doing, Don Jazzy revealed in an interview he granted Goldmynetv that he has not been to his house in a long time.

The Nigerian music don said:

“I spend more time in the studio, I have not been to my house in one year, I live in Nicon Town, but I have an eight bedroom office in Lekki. So I am always there, and I have cloths and everything there. I still want to work; I have two other producers’ in my camp. But then most of the music we put out today, you will see that it is produced by Don Jazzy.”

Meanwhile Don Jazzy some weeks back said he needs to marry this year, 2015. Who knows, his not staying at home might also be affecting his single status as no woman would want to be married and be living in a music studio.

Don Jazzy and D'banj stormed the Nigerian music scene in the early 2000s and they changed the tune of music Nigerians were dancing to and ever since then Don Jazzy have been on top of his game.
