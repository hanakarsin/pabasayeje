Neace - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-08-18

Popular Twitch streamer best known for his Fortnite and League of Legends streams. He has over 100,000 followers on the platform as well as over 330,000 subscribers on his eponymous YouTube channel. 

Before Fame

He's lived in Los Angeles, California. 

Trivia

He used to stream under the username BingeGaming, but changed all of his handles and websites to NEACE in 2017.

Family Life

He switched games from League of Legends to Fortnight in 2018, as well as planned a move across the country.

Associated With

He's often paired with fellow Fortnight player Tyler Blevins on streams.
