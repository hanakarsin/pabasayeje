Shannon Leto - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-03-03

Drummer known for being a member of the rock band 30 Seconds to Mars who has also pursued other artistic outlets, including abstract visual art and writing.

Before Fame

He appeared on an episode of My So-Called Life. He formed 30 Seconds to Mars in 1998.

Trivia

He has been influenced by Keith Moon. He founded the direct-trade coffee company Black Fuel Trading Company in 2014.

Family Life

His younger brother, musician and actor Jared Leto, is also in 30 Seconds to Mars. In 2020, he began dating actress Cara Santana.

Associated With

He and Tomo Milicevic have been bandmates in 30 Seconds to Mars.
