BBNaija S7: 'I Regret Saying Groovy Has Small Manhood'

Publish date: 2024-06-07

Disqualified Big Brother Naija Season 7 housemate, Beauty Tukura has expressed regrets over her recent outburst and body-shaming of her lover, Groovy.

Naija News recalls that on Saturday, Beauty while ending her relationship with Groovy for dancing with Chomzy mocked him for having small manhood.

The reality TV star angry said: “Tell that bastard with his small p3nis that if he calls my name, me and him will have a problem.”

Speaking during her BBNaija ‘unlocked’ interview, Beauty said she regrets her actions, stressing that the statement was made out of anger.

The former Miss Nigeria added that she apologised to Groovy on Sunday but she wished they had cleared the issue.

She said: “To be honest I would apologize for that. I will regret that I said that, it was said out of anger.

“I regret that I said that out of anger. I already apologized to him on Sunday but I wish we cleared the issue.”

I’m so sorry I left like this

Beauty earlier apologised to her fans over her early exit from the BBNaija reality TV show.

In a short video sighted online, Beauty expressed appreciation to those who supported her through her short stay in the house despite her shortcomings.

She further apologized for her irrational behaviour which led to her untimely exit from the Biggie house.

Beauty added that alcohol got the best of her and promised to act better.

She said: “To everyone who’s been riding with me or supporting me, thank you.

“I’m so sorry I left like this. Alcohol got the best of me. Beauty will do better.”
