Read This Important Update On NEXIT E-mail Activation Link

Publish date: 2024-08-09

The National Social Investment Programme (NSIP) has released an important update for Npower beneficiaries having difficulty with email activation links for their NEXIT registration.

The NEXIT portal IS for former Npower beneficiaries (batches A and B) to apply for Central Bank of Nigeria (CBN) Empowerment Options.

However, after the portal was launched, some of the applicants have been having issues completing their NEXIT registration process due to issues with their email activation link.

Such affected persons have now been instructed to forward their details to [email protected] in order to get the issues rectified.

The details are to be sent in the order and format below to [email protected]

N-Power Registration number
First Name
Middle Name
Surname
Date of Birth
BVN
State of Residence
LGA of residence
Address when registering for the N-Power program
Programme: (eg Nteach, Nhealth, Nagro)
Batch
Old Email Address
New Email address
Phone Number
