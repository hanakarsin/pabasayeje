Early life and education

Chidinma Ekile was born on the 2nd of May 1991, in Ketu in Lagos State. She is the sixth born in a family of seven children.

Not much is known about her childhood, especially her parents' and siblings' details. However, while growing up, she always had a passion for music and was a church choir member in their village.

She attended her primary and secondary education in Ketu before relocating to Ikorodu with her family.

In an interview, she disclosed that she took her studies seriously, but she was determined to spend her time on music instead of attending a university.

Rise to fame

The peak of her fame came in 2010 when she became the winner of Project Fame 3. Project Fame, also known as Project Fame West Africa or MTN Project Fame West Africa, is a music talent TV reality show filmed in Lagos and aired in the West African subcontinent.

This reality show gave her the much-needed musical experience and career advancement. Before she took part in Project Fame 3, she had always admired the show and always wanted to be a participant.

In 2010, Ekile dared to take part in the show's casting. The singer had to compete with over 8000 other singers who wanted to show their talents that year.

She got lucky and was chosen for training along with 17 other participants. They received training in vocal, oratory and choreography for two and half months, which enhanced her music career more.

The training helped her interact with various musicians who had already stormed the industry with their hits.

By the end of season 3 in 2010, she emerged as the winner, going home with a valuable prize of N2.5 million, a fancy 2011 Toyota RAV4, and a production deal.

After the delightful triumph, she began to work on her album. The singer collaborated with several producers, and each of them brought something unique to the overall result.

In February 2011, she released her first two singles. One of the singles, Jankoliko, was recorded by Sound Sultan. In October of the same year, she released two other singles, Kedike and Run Dia Mouth.

Ever since her first release, the singer has risen to great fame, releasing more amazing songs. In May 2013, Chidinma signed an endorsement deal with MTN Nigeria.

Collaborations with other talented singers and producers have allowed her to create truly high-quality music that her fans appreciate.
