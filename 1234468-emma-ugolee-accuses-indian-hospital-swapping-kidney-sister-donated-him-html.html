Emma Ugolee accuses Indian hospital of swapping kidney his sister donated for him

Publish date: 2024-07-03

- Nigerians lately are sharing their bitter experiences during medical excursion to India

- A man recently narrated how he lost his mum due to medical negligence of an Indian hospital

- Media personality battling kidney-related ailment reveals how the kidney donated to him by his sister was exchanged in an Indian hospital while a bad one was given to him

A Nigerian man, Tobi Marshal, had shared the medical travails that led to the painful and untimely death of his mother after what he narrated to be a case of medical negligence and practice from a hospital in India and the social media has been mourning Marshal loss.

A media personality who has been suffering from kidney related ailment, Emma Ugolee, has added his voice to that to Tobi Marshal about Nigerians going on medical tourism to India with the hope of a better treatment of the ailment they are suffering from.
