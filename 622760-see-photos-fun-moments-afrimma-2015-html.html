All The Fun Moments From AFRIMMA 2015 (PHOTOS)

Publish date: 2024-06-18

On Saturday, October 10, the second edition of the African Muzik Magazine Awards (AFRIMMA) was held in Dallas, USA.

It was compered by Nigerian comedian Basketmouth and Uganda's Anne Kansiime. Guests were thrilled all evening with music, glitz, glamour mixed with some comedy.

There were also performances from some notable African music stars such as Yemi Alade, Diamond Platnumz Yvonne Chaka Chaka and AKA.
