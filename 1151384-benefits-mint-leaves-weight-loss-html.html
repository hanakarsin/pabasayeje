Warning

Despite the fact that mint has a beneficial effect on health, there are contraindications to its use:

  • individual intolerance to menthol;
  • acid reflux;
  • lactation;
  • pregnancy (regular use of tea with mint may provoke relaxation of the uterine muscles and consequently lead to the threat of miscarriage);
  • men should also be wary of mint because it reduces reproductive function and potency;
  • mint tea is contraindicated for children under three years.

Any herbs can have a bad effect on the body in case you consume them too much. If you drink too much mint tea then this can cause:

  • drowsiness;
  • diarrhea;
  • pain in the muscles;
  • slowing of the heart rate;
  • convulsions.
