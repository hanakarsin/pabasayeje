Jesse Eisenberg - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-06-25

Best known for his Academy Award nominated role as Mark Zuckerberg in the 2010 film The Social Network. He has also starred in the films The Squid and the Whale, Adventureland, 30 Minutes or Less, Now You See Me, Zombieland and Batman v Superman: Dawn of Justice.

Before Fame

He made his first television appearance when he was 16 on Fox's Get Real. He had his first leading role in the 2002 film Rodger Dodger. 

Trivia

He played himself in the 2013 comedy film He's Way More Famous Than You. 

Family Life

His mother Amy dressed up as a clown at children's parties for a living and his father worked at a hospital. He dated Anna Strout from 2002 until they split in 2012. He then dated his The Double co-star Mia Wasikowska from 2013 to 2015. He later rekindled things with Strout; they married in 2017 and welcomed a son later that same year.

Associated With

He starred alongside Aziz Ansari in the 2011 comedy 30 Minutes Or Less.
