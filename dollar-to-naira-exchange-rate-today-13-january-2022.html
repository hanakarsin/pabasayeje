Dollar To Naira Exchange Rate Today 13 January 2022

Publish date: 2024-04-29

This is the news about Dollar to Naira rate at the official and black market exchange rate Today January 13, 2022.

Read Naija News update on the official dollar rates as well as Black Market rates, Bureau De Change (BDC) rates, and CBN rates.

How Much Is Dollar To Naira Exchange Rate Today Official Rate?

The official rate today, Thursday January 13th 2022, for $1 dollar to naira = ₦414.29/$1.

According to the data at the FMDQ Security Exchange where forex is traded officially, exchange rate between the naira and the US dollar opened at ₦414.29/$1 on Thursday 13th, after it closed at ₦414.11 to a $1 on Wednesday, 12th January 2022.

How much is exchange rate of Dollar to Naira in Black Market today?

The exchange rate for a dollar to naira at Lagos Parallel Market (Black Market) players buy a dollar for N567 and sell at N570 on Thursday, January 13th 2022, according to sources at Bureau De Change (BDC).

Please note that the Central Bank of Nigeria (CBN) does not recognise the parallel market (black market), as it has directed individuals who want to engage in Forex to approach their respective banks.
