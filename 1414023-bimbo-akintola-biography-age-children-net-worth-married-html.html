Bimbo Akintola biography

She was born in Ibadan, Oyo State, Nigeria to a Muslim cleric from Oyo State and a mother from Edo State. Bimbo is the third born in a family of six. Her passion for acting was clear from childhood, and through the support of her mother, she took part in plays.

Akintola is a beautiful, creative, and highly talented actress. She began acting at a tender age, during her time at school in end-of-year school events.
