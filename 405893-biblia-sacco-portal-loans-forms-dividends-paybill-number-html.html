Biblia Sacco: portal, loans, forms, dividends, paybill number

Publish date: 2024-04-29

Biblia Sacco is one of the many financial agencies in Kenya that offer loan products and other related services to citizens. The organization has been in existence since 1976, making it a reliable and sustainable outfit. Over the years, this organization has become one of the go-to institutions for its over 4000 members.

Biblia Sacco has its headquarters in Nairobi. However, it serves people in the entire country. The organization supports its members by mobilizing their savings and getting loans for worthwhile projects and investments. It helps to understand the operations of the Sacco if you want to gain.

Biblia Sacco loans

This is one of the most important products offered by Sacco. Other products from the group include a savings plan that helps members take charge of their finances and a housing plan. Even so, loans remain the primary product.

Before selecting a loan, make sure to understand the details. You may need to use Biblia Sacco loan calculator, or sit down with one of the agents to understand what you are getting yourself into. The loans differ in terms and conditions as follows below.

Normal or development loan

The loan is offered to members at a rate of three times their savings, as long as it does not exceed 3.5 million shillings. It also has a repayment period of up to 60 months, and the interest charged is a one percent reducing balance.

Emergency loan

Like it is with most financial institutions, the emergency loan is a common type of product. Biblia offers it within 48 hours, and it should be rapid in a maximum of 24 months.

Salary advance loan

This one works for members who pay their shares consistently and have a good loan repayment record. It operates under normal application terms, and advances are offered at a rate of half the amount of shares one owns.

Members are expected to pay this loan within a month to three months. To get it, you need to provide a three months payment slip and three months bank statement details.

Sinking fund loan

Normal terms apply in this case as long as members consistently contribute their shares and repay loans. The interest payable for this type of loan is 1.25% per month on a reducing balance rate.

Members are eligible for up to 300,000 Kenya shillings to be repaid in 24 months. A member's loan ration must also be lower than 60% to qualify for this type of loan.

Product loan

This is another popular loan type on Biblia Sacco. It is offered to members who want to buy household items, a car, or even land. The interest rate is a bit different as it is charged at 14% per annum. There is a year allowed for servicing the product.

Insurance premium finance

The application is made under normal terms. However, members must contribute shares and pay their loans consistently. The marked repayment period is often 12 months at a 1.25% interest rate monthly, on reducing balance. The maximum amount of loan offered depends on the premium payable, and cheques are written directly to the insurance company of choice.

Premium loan

This is a special kind of loan that is offered to members who pay their loans above the normal ceiling and at higher interest rates. You only apply after at least a year of surviving the development. Terms of application are also normal for all members interested in this type of loan.

How to join Biblia Sacco

To join this Sacco, one has to first meet certain basic requirements set by the organization. You must be a Christian or work for a Christian evangelical organization. The company you work for should have not less than six employees ready and willing to join Biblia Sacco.

The company should write a letter to the Sacco expressing their commitment to pay any amounts owed by their employee in case of termination. This money should come from the terminal benefit owed to the employee. Besides that, people eligible to become members should have the following traits.

  • Should be 18 years old and more
  • Must have a good character
  • Must reside within the area of the Sacco's operations
  • Must be willing and ready to follow Sacco's bylaws and guidelines diligently

Besides this, there is a unit affiliation application process that interested people can follow. In such a case, certain documents must be attached. Include the following information in your unit affiliation application.

Biblia Sacco portal

To access the portal services of Biblia Sacco, certain forms must be submitted for approval. To register for the member portal, first, download Biblia Sacco forms. This is the member data form that you will fill and submit via email or drop the filled form physically at any of the branch's offices. To register:

  • First, open your preferred browser on PC or phone
  • Click on the Sacco's portal and click enter or go
  • The Login page comes up
  • Proceed to click on the tab, Register a new membership
  • Key in the relevant membership number such as PF Number
  • Add your national ID Number and click to submit
  • Once done, a new window opens
  • A message is then sent from the Sacco bearing the start key.
  • Enter this key, which is a 4-digit number that is sent to your mobile number. Proceed to create and confirm your password. To complete the process of registration.
