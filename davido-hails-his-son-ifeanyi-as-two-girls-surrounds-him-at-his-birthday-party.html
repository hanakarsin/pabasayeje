Davido Hails His Son, Ifeanyi As Two Girls Surrounds Him At His Birthday Party

Publish date: 2024-07-05

Nigerian singer, David Adeleke, popularly called Davido, threw a lavish birthday party, on Sunday, for his son Ifeanyi.

Naija News recalls that the son of the singer celebrated his third birthday on Thursday, October 20, 2022.

Taking to his Instagram stories, Davido shared adorable moments from the party, which was attended by many Nigerian celebrities and friends.

One of the footage shows Davido’s son surrounded by two girls at the party and the OBO crooner hailed him saying “Big Boy, only you, only you two”

Davido Includes His Second Son In Family Portrait

Meanwhile, Davido has reportedly accepted the paternity of his second son, Dawson.

This is coming barely a month after American-based makeup artist, Larissa London, and Davido linked up for the first time since she welcomed her baby.

The singer on Sunday, took to his Instagram page to show off the portraits of his father’s grandchildren.

Surprisingly, the photo of Dawson was included and the development has generated a lot of reactions.

He captioned the adorable video: “Papas grandkids”

Many netizens expressed excitement while applauding Davido for taking responsibility for the child.
