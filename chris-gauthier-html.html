Chris Gauthier - Trivia, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-03-15

Veteran character actor known for his work in genre entertainment. He appeared as Smee on Once Upon a Time in Wonderland, Phil in Netflix's A Series of Unfortunate Events, and Vincent in Eureka. He amassed over 100 different acting roles throughout his prolific on-screen career. 

Before Fame

He was born in Dunstable, England, but moved to Canada when he was five. 

Trivia

His feature films have included Watchmen and Freddy vs. Jason. He passed away at the young age of 48. 

Family Life

His grandfather and uncle were both artists. He was married and had two children.

Associated With

He starred alongside actors the likes of Neil Patrick Harris, Robert Englund, and Jeffrey Dean Morgan during his decades-long career.
