Charlie Gillespie - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-03-09

Actor who became best known for his starring role as Luke, the lead singer of the band Sunset Curve, in the Netflix series Julie and the Phantoms. 

Before Fame

He made his feature film debut in the 2014 Canadian film The Outlaw League. 

Trivia

He appeared as the character Brian in two episodes of the CW series Charmed in 2018. That same year, he also had a recurring role as the character Brody Johnson on the series 2nd Generation. 

Family Life

He was raised in Dieppe, New Brunswick, Canada. He moved from Canada to the United States at a young age. His mother's name is Jeanette. He is one of five siblings; his brothers are named Michael, Patrick, and Ryan and his sister's name is Meghan.

Associated With

In Julie and the Phantoms, he stars alongside Madison Reyes and Owen Joyner.
