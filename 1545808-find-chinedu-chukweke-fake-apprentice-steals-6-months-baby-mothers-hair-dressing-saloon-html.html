Publish date: 2024-06-30
  • A child has been stolen from his mother, who operates a hair salon at Kure Ultra Modern Market, Minna, Niger state
  • The child named Chinedu Chukwueke was whisked away by a lady who pretended that she came to learn hairdressing
  • The father of the baby boy, Chikezie Stanley Chuks, said on Facebook that the baby was taken on Sunday, July 23

A fake apprentice has stolen a baby from his mother, who runs a hair-dressing salon in Niger state.

The child, Chinedu Chukwueke, was whisked away by the lady who came in the guise of learning work at his mother's salon at Kure Ultra Modern Market, Minna.

Chikezie Stanley Chuks, father to the 6-month-old baby, raised the alarm in a Facebook post.
