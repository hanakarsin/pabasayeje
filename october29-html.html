October 29 Horoscope and Zodiac

Publish date: 2024-03-26

Pluto is the planetary ruler of your sign, but as you were born in the first Decan, or part, of the sign, Pluto's influence over you is twice as strong. Pluto, being the planet of transformation, is responsible for your need for power and renewal. You are rarely content in your current position, instead wanting the best from all aspects of life. You may be most interested in emotional and financial, but be careful not to grow obsessive or compulsive in your pursuits. Attempt to embrace your compassion in your personal relationships, as you may be tempted to use your social gifts for manipulation and personal gain.
