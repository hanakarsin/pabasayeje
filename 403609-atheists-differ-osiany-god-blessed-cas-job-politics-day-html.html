Atheists in Kenya have dismissed David Osiany's sentiments that it is by the grace of God he landed the plum chief administrative secretary (CAS) job.

Following his appointment, Osiany had linked his success to the prayers and support of his wife Syombua Osiany.

However, in a quick rejoinder, the nonbelievers through their Twitter on Sunday, February 21, said he was elected because of the politics of the day and his appointment had nothing to do with the prayers of his wife.

The communication strategist was appointed the Trade and Enterprise Development CAS by President Uhuru Kenyatta in his recent cabinet reshuffle.

Prior to his appointment, in 2012, he served in the office of the Prime Minister as a Youth Affairs advisor and is reported to have built a strong relationship with Raila Odinga since then.

Syombua's congratulatory message

Osiany together with her media personality wife are known to be devout Christians.

Syombua in her congratulatory message likened her husband's excellent skills with a passage in the bible talking about talent.

"Proverbs 22:29. You see a man truly skilled in his work? He will serve kings. Congratulations to you Love @DavidOsiany HSC, on your appointment as the Chief Administrative Secretary (CAS) in the Ministry of Industrialization, Trade and Enterprise Development,'' she said.

In other cabinet changes, Basic Education Principal Secretary Belio Kipsang moved to State Department for Regional and Northern Corridor Development.
