Jack Skye - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-02-27

Children's toys and games content creator who is recognized for having been a member of Kids Fun TV's Fun Squad. He also published content on his own self titled channel. His channel has included adventure vlogs, challenge videos and prank videos. 

Before Fame

One of his first videos was inspired by the reality show competition American Ninja Warrior.

Trivia

He participated in the bottle flip challenge in a video viewed more than 900,000 times. He enjoys playing Minecraft, scootering, making music and toys. 

Family Life

His parents monitored and ran admin for his social media accounts. His sister is Jazzy Skye.

Associated With

He was a fan of fellow creator Paxton Myler.
