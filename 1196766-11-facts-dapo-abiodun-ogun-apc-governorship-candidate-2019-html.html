11 facts you should know about Dapo Abiodun, Ogun APC governorship candidate for 2019

Publish date: 2024-05-06

The National Working Committee (NWC) of the All Progressives Congress (APC) has declared Dapo Abiodun the winner of the Ogun state governorship primary.

He was declared the winner after polling 102,305 votes, against his closest contender, Jimi Lawal, who polled 51,153.

News of his winning the governorship ticket was contained in a statement signed by Yekini Nabena, acting national publicity secretary of the APC.

If elected in the forthcoming 2019 general elections, Dapo Abiodun will take over the Ogun state governorship seat from Governor Ibikunle Amosun.

Here are 14 key facts about Dapo Abiodun, the official flag bearer of the ruling APC in the 2019 gubernatorial election in Ogun state.

Family Background

1. Dapo is a native of Iperu-Remo in Ogun state and belongs to a royal family

2. Prince Abiodun was born by Dr Emmanuel and Mrs Victoria Abiodun from Iperu-Remo, in Ogun East senatorial district on May 29, 1960

3. He is the father of late Olugbenga Abiodun aka DJ Olu, a close friend of the Nigerian Music star Davido

Education

4. He studied civil engineering at the then University of Ife (now, Obafemi Awolowo University), Ife, Osun state and subsequently, Accounting at Kennesaw State University, Atlanta Georgia, United States of America (USA)

5. He holds a Honours et Doctorate Degree in Finance from Ekiti State University and another in Business Administration from Adeleke University, Ede, Osun state.

Profession

6. He is the managing director/CEO of Heyden Petroleum Ltd [HPL], one of the largest indigenous players in the downstream, midstream, and upstream oil and gas industry in Nigeria

7. He is also reportedly the founder of First Power Limited, among others

Politics

8. Dapo Abiodun is a founding member of Peoples Democratic Party (PDP) in Ogun state

9. He contested the Ogun East senatorial seat on the platform of the PDP in 2015 general elections
