Nairobi's Britam Tower featured among top 10 skyscrapers in the world

Publish date: 2024-07-12

Rising from Nairobi's skyline at 200 metres, Britam Tower, in Upperhill has been featured among 10 high-rise buildings globally.

The magnificent skyscraper received the Emporis Skyscraper Award making it the only skyscraper in Africa to feature among the top 10 buildings in the award’s nine-year history.

The award is the world’s most renowned prize for high-rise architecture where winners are chosen by a jury of architectural experts from all over the world.

However, the nominated buildings are judged based on their achievements in functions and aesthetics.

“Britam Tower by GAPP Architects and Triad Architects was the first skyscraper in Africa to feature in the top 10 Emporis Skyscraper Award’s list," Emporis said in a statement.

"It made its way to the 10th position among other outstanding skyscrapers in the world,” the award organisers added.

Britam Tower, developed by Britam Properties Limited, is the tallest building in East and Central Africa and is the insurance company's flagship project for its property strategy.

It was assessed among other buildings in Europe, Asia and the United States that included Generali Tower in Milan, Chicago’s 150 North Riverside and Riverpoint.

The top spot went to the Lotte World Tower in Seoul, South Korea.

While accepting the prize, Triad, the firm behind the tower's design noted it would continue to design award-winning buildings in Africa.

“Triad Architects takes pride in leading the design and construction processes of Britam Tower. The firm continues to design award-winning buildings in Africa," said Triad.

Our approach is to design creative and unique spaces that meet current international standards and are infused with an elegant local flair.“We are proud to be associated with this prestigious list of outstanding skyscrapers,” the firm added.

Britam embarked on its property and real estate strategy in 2011 as part of the Group’s diversification strategy to provide real estate exposure to investing clients.
