Stephanie Ruhle - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-05-03

News anchor known for her work on NBC news as well as MSNBC Live. She is also known for having been the editor-at-large for Bloomberg News and co-hosted their television program Bloomberg GO. She has interviewed a wide range of presidents, celebrities, and other influential figures. 

Before Fame

She graduated from Lehigh University where she earned her BA in International Business. Prior to her work with Bloomberg, she worked for Merrill Lynch and Credit Suisse. She gained major headlines in 2012 when she helped break the story of London Whale, the head trader behind the 2012 JP Morgan Chase trading loss. 

Trivia

She is also known for having founded the Corporate Investment Bank or CIB Women's Network and was also the co-chair of the Women on Wall Street steering community. 

Family Life

She married her husband Andy Hubbard and together they have three children. 

Associated With

She has hosted interviews with a wide range of political figures and prominent socialites including Donald Trump prior to his election, Kanye West, and Martha Stewart.
