Lyssy Noel - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-03-14

Avid social media user across almost every network who has earned over 2.2 million subscribers to her Lyssy Noel channel, where she primarily posts arcade videos. She also has a second channel called LyssysLife where she posts vlogs, tutorials and more. She has posted multiple videos at Chuck E. Cheese. 

Before Fame

She created her YouTube channel with the username TwilightClover98 in September of 2010, uploading her first vlog, "Adventures with Lyssy Las Vegas," in July of 2014. 

Trivia

She has gained thousands of fans on YouNow and Instagram in addition to YouTube. Her hobbies growing up included dog training and playing tennis. Her dog, Angel Noel, has had its own Instagram page that has gained over 20.4 thousand followers as of November, 2021. 

Family Life

Her real name is Alyssa Noel, she was born in Anaheim, California, and later resided in Minnesota. Her dad left soon after she was born, but her mother remarried when Lyssy was five. She has a younger half-sister.

Associated With

Eva Gutowski inspired her to start her YouTube channel.
